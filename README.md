# Steven Denney

**Assistant Professor of International Relations and Korean Studies**
Leiden University

I bridge digital humanities with quantitative social science to study national identity, migration, and political change.

## Research Focus

- Survey experiments and experimental designs
- Machine learning and computational text analysis
- Large language models for political discourse
- Nations, nationalism, and immigration attitudes

## Current Projects

- [**Fairness or Threat?**](https://github.com/scdenney/nwo26-immigration-backlash) — Understanding sources of immigration backlash across democracies
- **Contesting the Nation** — Digital pipelines for analyzing national history textbooks
- **Nationalism and Political Change** — How institutions shape national identity
